Understanding the Impact of Pollution on Our Environment

Air Pollution

Air pollution occurs when harmful substances, such as particulate matter or gases, are introduced into the atmosphere, causing damage to living organisms and the environment.

Water Pollution

Water pollution is the contamination of water bodies, such as rivers, lakes, and oceans, by pollutants, leading to adverse effects on aquatic life and human health.

Land Pollution

Land pollution involves the degradation of the Earth's surface through the misuse of natural resources, leading to the contamination of soil and the destruction of ecosystems.
